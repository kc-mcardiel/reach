<h3>By making a contribution to the REACH, you are improving people's access to the power of performing arts.</h3>
The <em>Building the Future</em> Capital Campaign will raise $250 million in support of the design, construction, and associated costs of the REACH, with a portion dedicated to ongoing and future programming. The project is supported exclusively by private contributions and every gift, of every amount, is vital to our success. We invite you and others who have been impacted by the arts to make a gift—of any magnitude—to help build this artistic new campus.

<strong><a href="https://cms.kennedy-center.org/support/reach-support/">Become a part of this historic expansion by making your gift today!</a></strong>
